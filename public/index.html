ABESTAND Empowering Agriculture through Increased Standard Accessibility

Welcome to ABESTAND!

ABESTAND is a dedicated web portal designed to provide centralized, organized, and easily accessible references to Philippine Agricultural and Biosystems Engineering (ABE)-related standards. Tailored for ABE students, educators, professionals, and board exam reviewees, the portal offers a user-friendly interface, mobile-responsive design, and categorized access to standards from sources such as AMTEC and BAFS. With its structured layout, category filter, and basic search functionality, ABESTAND supports efficient study, research, and technical referencingâ€”particularly for users in academic and rural settings who require fast and reliable access to essential documents.

Pradilla, a student of the Department of Agricultural and Biosystems Engineering at Visayas State University (VSU-DABE), as part of his undergraduate thesis. ABESTAND is continuously being improved and updated as more standards are published and made available.
